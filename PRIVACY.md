# Privacy Policy for Chaoxing Helper

**Last Updated: February 17, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Chaoxing Helper** ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and discloses your information in connection with your use of our Chrome browser extension (the "Extension").

## 1. Data Collection and Usage
The Extension is designed to help users manage their own quiz data on specific educational platforms (*.chaoxing.com and *.erya100.com).

* **Quiz Data**: The Extension scrapes quiz questions and options from the web pages you visit. This data is processed **locally** on your device to generate Excel/Word files for your personal use.
* **User Credentials**: The Extension operates within your active browser session. It **does not** collect, store, or transmit your passwords or login credentials.
* **Local Storage**: Temporary data (such as scraped questions) is stored in your browser's local storage (`chrome.storage.local`) solely for the purpose of maintaining state between page navigations.

## 2. Data Sharing and Transfer
**We do not collect any personal data.**
* **No Remote Servers**: All data processing happens locally on your computer.
* **No Third-Party Sharing**: We do not transfer, sell, or share your data with any third parties, advertisers, or external servers.

## 3. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you by updating the date at the top of this policy.
